Jenny Yee

Jenny Yee is a graphic designer / art director based in Los Angeles. She received her BFA from Art Center College of Design in 2004. Brands that she has worked on include: Bacara, CalTech, Clockshop, Disney, DoubleTree, Hilton, Museum of Contemporary Arts, Los Angeles, Pinkberry, Skechers, Sprinkles Cupcakes, Strange Invisible Perfumes, and UCLA. In her spare time, she enjoys traveling, hiking, and capturing each moment with her camera.
